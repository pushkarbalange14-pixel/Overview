Operations Manual

01

Watch the Grid

Five stations keep the facility alive: Power, Security, Comms, Life Support and the Data Core. Each has an integrity meter. Left unattended, incidents drain it toward zero.

02

Read the Incident

When a station flags an incident, select it. If the type is unknown, run a Scan to reveal it — free, but it costs precious seconds.

03

Choose the Response

Four responses are available: Stabilize, Reroute, Purge, and Lockdown. Each counters specific incident types. The wrong call costs Focus and integrity.

04

Spend Focus Wisely

Every action costs Focus Points, which regenerate slowly. Running dry leaves you unable to respond — manage your reserve like ammunition.

05

Clear Directives

Each shift hands you live directives — hold thresholds, clear incidents cleanly, survive spikes. Clearing all directives closes the shift and raises your clearance.

06

Don't Lose the Facility

If overall facility integrity collapses to zero, the shift ends in a meltdown. Chain clean resolutions to build your multiplier and post a better score.
